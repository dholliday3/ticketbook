Just like in linear or notion, in the list mode, we should be able to open tickets in tabs so you can toggle between tabs, close them, pin them, and edit different tickets or plans.

You can view in \~/workspace/resources/posthog the posthog product that has the ability to open multiple tabs. We can draw some inspiration from them, while still maintaining an approach that makes sense for us. Don't over index on the posthog example as it might not apply, but the interaction patterns and even spacing are a nice inspo. Maybe some of the implementation will help as well.
